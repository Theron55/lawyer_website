Features

Built around the way immigration firms work

Instead of generic CRMs, Intake Automation focuses on the two things that create the most friction in immigration cases: getting accurate information and getting complete documents.

Intake without back-and-forth

Mobile-first client intake

Clients complete a guided intake that uses your questions and your case types—no PDF forms, no copy-pasting from long emails.

Documents in one place

Structured document collection

Passport, I-94, visas, marriage evidence, financials, police records—clients upload everything to the right “bucket”, so your team doesn’t have to hunt through attachments.

AI assistance, not auto-lawyering

Case summaries you can trust

Intake data is turned into a short summary with key facts, risk flags, and suggested next steps. You stay in control, AI just gets you to the starting point faster.

Workflow

How it works with your firm

We don’t replace your existing systems—we clean up the messy middle between “I think I want to hire you” and “this file is ready to prepare.”

Step 1

Share your intake link

You send your branded intake link from your website, a text message, or your consultation reminder. Clients choose their language and complete a guided questionnaire in one sitting.

Step 2

Client uploads the right documents

For each case type, you define which documents you want to see. Clients upload from their phone or computer; everything is organized by category so your team sees what’s there and what’s missing at a glance.

Step 3

You review a clean case summary

Before your consult, you open a dashboard with key facts, a short AI summary written for attorneys, and a list of missing items—so you can spend time advising instead of extracting information.
