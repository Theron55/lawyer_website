What we automate for your firm

Purpose-built for immigration practices — from first contact through document collection and internal review.

Intake automation

Smart online intake

Clients complete a clean, guided intake form. The system generates a concise case summary with risk flags (overstay, prior removal, criminal history) for your quick review.

  • Immigration-specific intake questions
  • Automatic case summaries for attorneys
  • All leads captured in one dashboard
Document workflow

Document requests & uploads

After intake, clients receive a customized document checklist and secure upload link. Files land in organized folders tied to each case.

  • Case-specific document lists
  • Secure upload page branded to your firm
  • Files routed into structured folders
Case pipeline

Simple pipeline tracking

See every matter at a glance: intake status, documents in, what’s missing, and what needs your attention today.

  • New lead → Ready for filing, in one view
  • Clear “next action” for each case
  • Fewer matters stalling due to missing documents

How it works

A lightweight system that plugs into your existing tools. No new logins for your clients — no complicated software for your staff.
